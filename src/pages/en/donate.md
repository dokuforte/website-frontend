Your support makes it possible for current and future generations to better understand the history of Israel and the Jewish peoplehood. Our photo archive is completely free and publicly accessible. To keep it that way, and to continue expanding our collections, we rely on contributions from people who believe that personal and collective memory matter.

Your gift will enable:

- The continued growth of our collections documenting Jewish and Israeli life
- The discovery and preservation of privately held photo collections
- The digitization of photographs
- Free access for educators, researchers, and the public

### Mid-level & partnership giving

We are currently expanding our mid-level giving program for individuals and families who wish to play a deeper role in safeguarding the visual heritage of Israel-Diaspora relations.

Mid-level supporters receive:

- Personalized updates about preserved collections
- Early access to selected digitized materials
- Recognition in our annual report

If you would like to become part of this circle, please contact our founder and executive director, Noa Schiller at: noa.schiller@dokuforte.com.

### Volunteering

Our success relies on dedicated volunteers who share a passion for history, heritage, and an interest in photography. From discovering new private collections to helping expand and process our archive, we regularly welcome volunteers from around the world. No prior archival or photographic experience is required; we provide training and guidance throughout. If you are interested in volunteering, please contact us at: archive@dokuforte.com.

### Method of collecting donations

Donations are processed through the following payment service provider:

- Barion
- PayPal

Donations may be made through:

- the bank card payment interface provided by Barion Payment Zrt. and PayPal Holdings Inc.

### Personal data

By making a donation, you consent to the storage of the personal data necessary to process your contribution. We collect the following information:

- email address
- donation amount
- date of donation

Further details regarding data storage and processing can be found in our data management guidelines.

### Withdrawing a donation

Every donor has the right to request a refund within 7 days of payment if the donation was made by mistake. To request a refund, please contact us at: archive@dokuforte.com. Please include the following text in the subject line: “Donation withdrawal”. Refunds are processed within a maximum of 30 days.

### Payment security

We ensure the security of online payments through a secure SSL certificate, similar to those used by banks. All transmitted information is encrypted. Online card payments are processed via Barion or via PayPal. We don’t receive or store bank card data.
